Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to Flovoo, a unified business messaging platform. It explains how we collect, use, process, and protect information from visitors to our website and registered customers using the Flovoo platform.

1. Who We Are

Flovoo is a business messaging and customer communications platform designed for SMBs in the MENA region. Flovoo consolidates conversations from WhatsApp Business API, Facebook Messenger, Instagram, SMS, Live Chat, and other channels into a single shared inbox.

Flovoo is operated by Flovoo Technologies W.L.L., a company registered in the State of Qatar.

For privacy-related matters contact: privacy@flovoo.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account & Registration Data

  • Full name, work email address, and password
  • Company name, business type, and team size
  • Phone number and country
  • Billing information (processed securely by Stripe)

2.2 Usage & Platform Data

  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Pages visited and timestamps
  • Feature usage and team activity
  • Performance and error reporting information

2.3 Customer Conversation & Contact Data

Flovoo acts as a data processor on behalf of customers. Conversations, contact records, tags, and messages belong to our customers and are processed solely to provide the service.

Note: Flovoo does not apply artificial intelligence or machine learning to the content of customer conversations.

2.4 Payment Data

Payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. Flovoo does not store credit card numbers, CVV codes, or complete payment card details.

2.5 Communications Data

If you contact our support team, we may retain communications to provide assistance and improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide access to the platform
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Improve performance and reliability
  • Provide support and onboarding
  • Detect fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send service-related notifications and updates

We do not sell personal information and do not use customer conversation data for advertising or profiling.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • User consent where required

5. Data Sharing & Sub-processors

We work with trusted service providers to operate Flovoo.

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) – Cloud infrastructure and storage
  • Stripe, Inc. – Payment processing
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. – WhatsApp, Messenger and Instagram integrations
  • Twilio, Inc. – SMS delivery services

We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect users and our services.

6. International Data Transfers

Flovoo is headquartered in Qatar. Data may be processed and stored in the United States and/or the European Union through our cloud service providers.

Appropriate safeguards including Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) and Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) are implemented where applicable.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data during active subscription and a limited retention period after closure
  • Conversation data according to the subscribed plan
  • Billing records as required by law
  • Log data for security and diagnostics purposes

After retention periods expire, information is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Rights

  • Right to Access
  • Right to Rectification
  • Right to Erasure
  • Right to Object
  • Right to Portability
  • Right to Restrict Processing

To exercise any privacy right, contact: privacy@flovoo.com

9. Cookies & Tracking

  • Essential cookies required for platform functionality
  • Analytics cookies for website insights
  • Preference cookies for language and display settings

Users may manage non-essential cookies through browser settings.

The Flovoo platform uses only functional cookies required for operation.

10. Security

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit
  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular security reviews
  • AWS-hosted infrastructure

While we maintain strong safeguards, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

11. Children's Privacy

Flovoo is intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18 years of age.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be posted on this page and may be communicated by email or in-product notifications.

We typically respond within 2–3 business days.
